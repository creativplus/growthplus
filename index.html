Questions Founders Ask Us

How is this different from other agencies?
Most agencies are "button pushers." We are a growth division (Digi Up Solution) powered by AI. We don't just look at ads; we look at your offer, landing page, and unit economics. Plus, our AI systems allow us to analyze data faster than humanly possible.
What budgets do you work with?
We typically work with brands spending a minimum of ₹50k - ₹1L/month on ads, or businesses with strong organic revenue ready to turn on paid traffic. If you have no budget, we recommend our DIY AI tools instead.
Do you guarantee results?
Any agency guaranteeing ROAS without seeing your account is lying. We guarantee a proven system, transparent data, and relentless testing. If a campaign isn't working, we catch it in days, not months.
How fast can we scale?
It depends on your inventory and cash flow. Once we find a winning creative-audience combination (usually within 14-21 days), we can scale spend by 20-30% every few days while maintaining profitability.
